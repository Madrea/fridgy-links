Politica de Confidentialitate - Fridgy

Ultima actualizare: 20 mai 2026

Aceasta Politica de Confidentialitate descrie modul in care aplicatia Fridgy colecteaza, utilizeaza si protejeaza datele cu caracter personal ale utilizatorilor sai.

Prin utilizarea aplicatiei Fridgy, sunteti de acord cu practicile descrise in aceasta politica.

1. Cine suntem

Aplicatia Fridgy este operata de:

MADREA MIRCEA MATEI PERSOANA FIZICA AUTORIZATA

Romania

Email de contact: contact@fridgy.org

In sensul legislatiei privind protectia datelor cu caracter personal, noi suntem operatorul de date.

2. Ce date colectam

Colectam doar datele strict necesare pentru functionarea aplicatiei.

2.1. Date de cont

  • adresa de email
  • nume de utilizator sau nume afisat
  • metoda de autentificare (email/parola sau cont Google)

2.2. Date introduse de utilizator in aplicatie

  • produse adaugate in aplicatie
  • date de expirare ale produselor
  • setari ale aplicatiei (limba, preferinte de notificare)

2.3. Date tehnice

  • token-uri pentru notificari push
  • identificatori tehnici necesari functionarii aplicatiei
  • date despre utilizarea aplicatiei (ecrane vizitate, actiuni efectuate)
  • inregistrari ale sesiunilor de utilizare (interactiunile cu interfata, fara inregistrarea datelor sensibile precum parole sau date de plata)
  • informatii despre dispozitiv (model, sistem de operare, versiune aplicatie)
  • limba si tara dispozitivului

Nu colectam date sensibile, precum:

  • date medicale
  • date de card sau bancare
  • date biometrice
  • locatie precisa

Preturile produselor introduse de utilizator sunt stocate in contul sau si nu sunt partajate cu terti.

3. Scopul prelucrarii datelor

Datele colectate sunt utilizate exclusiv pentru:

  • crearea si administrarea contului de utilizator
  • furnizarea functionalitatilor principale ale aplicatiei
  • salvarea si gestionarea produselor utilizatorului
  • trimiterea notificarilor legate de expirarea produselor
  • functionarea tehnica si securitatea aplicatiei
  • imbunatatirea experientei utilizatorului

4. Temeiul legal al prelucrarii

Datele cu caracter personal sunt prelucrate in baza urmatoarelor temeiuri legale:

  • executarea serviciului furnizat prin aplicatia Fridgy
  • consimtamantul utilizatorului
  • interesul legitim de a asigura securitatea, stabilitatea si buna functionare a aplicatiei

Atunci cand prelucrarea se bazeaza pe consimtamant, utilizatorul isi poate retrage consimtamantul in orice moment, fara a afecta legalitatea prelucrarii efectuate anterior retragerii acestuia.

5. Stocarea si securitatea datelor

Datele cu caracter personal sunt stocate pe servere securizate si sunt protejate prin:

  • acces restrictionat
  • masuri tehnice si organizatorice adecvate
  • comunicare criptata (HTTPS)

Depunem eforturi rezonabile pentru a proteja datele personale impotriva accesului neautorizat, pierderii, distrugerii sau divulgarii acestora.

6. Partajarea datelor

Nu vindem, nu inchiriem si nu transferam datele personale ale utilizatorilor catre terti in scopuri comerciale.

Datele pot fi prelucrate de furnizori de servicii de incredere exclusiv in scopuri tehnice, cum ar fi:

  • servicii de gazduire (hosting)
  • servicii de livrare a notificarilor push (Expo, Apple, Google)
  • servicii AI de extragere text (Google Cloud Vision, Google Gemini)
  • servicii de procesare plati (RevenueCat, Apple, Google)
  • servicii de analiza si statistici de utilizare (Firebase Analytics de la Google, PostHog)
  • servicii de raportare a erorilor si performantei (Firebase Crashlytics)

Imaginile bonurilor fiscale sunt procesate prin servicii AI (Google Cloud Vision, Google Gemini) exclusiv pentru extragerea textului. Imaginile nu sunt stocate permanent pe serverele noastre.

Platile pentru abonamentul Premium sunt procesate prin RevenueCat si Apple/Google. Nu avem acces la datele cardului tau.

Acesti furnizori au acces doar la datele strict necesare pentru furnizarea serviciilor respective si sunt obligati sa respecte confidentialitatea datelor.

6.1. Analytics si inregistrarea sesiunilor

Pentru a intelege cum este utilizata aplicatia si pentru a o imbunatati continuu, folosim doua servicii de analiza:

Firebase Analytics (Google Ireland Limited)

Colecteaza date anonime despre utilizarea aplicatiei:

  • ecranele vizitate si actiunile efectuate
  • frecventa de utilizare a functionalitatilor
  • rapoarte de eroare si performanta
  • informatii tehnice despre dispozitiv

Datele sunt stocate pe infrastructura Google Cloud in Uniunea Europeana.

Politica de confidentialitate Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

PostHog (PostHog Inc.)

Colecteaza date despre interactiunea utilizatorului cu aplicatia, inclusiv inregistrari ale sesiunilor de utilizare. Inregistrarile sunt similare unor materiale video care arata navigarea prin aplicatie, exclusiv interfata aplicatiei Fridgy.

Inregistrarile NU contin:

  • continutul campurilor de parola (mascat automat)
  • date de plata (procesate de Apple/Google in dialoguri native, in afara aplicatiei)
  • continutul altor aplicatii de pe dispozitiv
  • imagini din camera sau galerie in afara contextului aplicatiei

Doar aproximativ 25% din sesiuni sunt inregistrate, selectate aleator, pentru a respecta volumul de date colectate.

Datele sunt stocate pe infrastructura PostHog Cloud in Uniunea Europeana (Frankfurt, Germania).

Politica de confidentialitate PostHog: https://posthog.com/privacy

Dezactivare

Utilizatorii pot solicita oprirea colectarii datelor de analiza si a inregistrarii sesiunilor contactandu-ne la contact@fridgy.org. Vom procesa cererea in maxim 30 de zile, conform GDPR.

7. Transferuri de date in afara Uniunii Europene

Datele cu caracter personal sunt stocate, in principal, in Uniunea Europeana. Furnizorii nostri de servicii (Firebase, PostHog, RevenueCat) folosesc infrastructura UE pentru stocarea principala a datelor.

In situatii limitate, anumite date tehnice pot fi prelucrate de serviciile partenere in afara UE (de exemplu, pentru anumite operatiuni ale Google sau Apple). In astfel de cazuri, ne asiguram ca sunt aplicate garantii adecvate pentru protectia datelor, conform Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) sau alte mecanisme prevazute de GDPR.

8. Notificari push

Aplicatia Fridgy poate trimite notificari push legate de produsele utilizatorului si datele de expirare.

Notificarile:

  • sunt optionale
  • pot fi dezactivate oricand din setarile aplicatiei sau ale dispozitivului

9. Perioada de pastrare a datelor

Datele cu caracter personal sunt pastrate:

  • pe durata existentei contului de utilizator
  • pana cand utilizatorul isi sterge contul

Dupa stergerea contului, datele sunt eliminate permanent din sistemele noastre, cu exceptia situatiilor in care legislatia aplicabila impune pastrarea anumitor informatii pentru o perioada determinata.

10. Drepturile utilizatorilor

Conform legislatiei privind protectia datelor cu caracter personal, utilizatorii beneficiaza de urmatoarele drepturi:

  • dreptul de acces la datele lor
  • dreptul de rectificare a datelor
  • dreptul de stergere a contului si a datelor asociate
  • dreptul de retragere a consimtamantului
  • dreptul de a solicita informatii privind prelucrarea datelor

Pentru exercitarea acestor drepturi, ne puteti contacta la contact@fridgy.org.

Utilizatorii au, de asemenea, dreptul de a depune o plangere la autoritatea competenta in materie de protectia datelor:

Autoritatea Nationala de Supraveghere a Prelucrarii Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP)

Website: https://www.dataprotection.ro

11. Stergerea contului

Utilizatorii isi pot sterge contul direct din aplicatie, din sectiunea de setari.

Prin stergerea contului:

  • toate datele personale asociate sunt eliminate permanent
  • accesul la aplicatie este revocat

12. Modificari ale politicii de confidentialitate

Ne rezervam dreptul de a actualiza aceasta Politica de Confidentialitate.

In cazul modificarilor semnificative, utilizatorii vor fi informati prin aplicatie sau prin alte mijloace adecvate.

Data ultimei actualizari va fi afisata in partea de sus a documentului.

13. Contact

Pentru orice intrebari sau solicitari legate de aceasta Politica de Confidentialitate sau de datele cu caracter personal, ne puteti contacta la:

contact@fridgy.org

Privacy Policy - Fridgy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Fridgy application collects, uses, and protects the personal data of its users.

By using the Fridgy application, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who we are

The Fridgy application is operated by:

MADREA MIRCEA MATEI PERSOANA FIZICA AUTORIZATA

Romania

Contact email: contact@fridgy.org

For the purposes of data protection legislation, we are the data controller.

2. What data we collect

We collect only the data strictly necessary for the functioning of the application.

2.1. Account data

  • email address
  • username or display name
  • authentication method (email/password or Google account)

2.2. Data entered by the user in the application

  • products added to the application
  • product expiration dates
  • application settings (language, notification preferences)

2.3. Technical data

  • tokens for push notifications
  • technical identifiers required for the functioning of the application
  • application usage data (screens visited, actions performed)
  • session recordings of usage (interface interactions, without recording sensitive data such as passwords or payment details)
  • device information (model, operating system, application version)
  • device language and country

We do not collect sensitive data such as:

  • medical data
  • credit card or banking data
  • biometric data
  • precise location

Product prices entered by the user are stored within their account and are not shared with third parties.

3. Purpose of data processing

The data collected is used exclusively for:

  • creating and managing the user account
  • providing the core functionalities of the application
  • storing and managing the user's products
  • sending notifications regarding product expiration
  • technical functioning and security of the application
  • improving the user experience

4. Legal basis for processing

Personal data is processed on the following legal grounds:

  • performance of the service provided through the Fridgy application
  • user consent
  • legitimate interest in ensuring the security, stability, and proper functioning of the application

Where processing is based on consent, the user may withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

5. Data storage and security

Personal data is stored on secure servers and protected through:

  • restricted access
  • appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • encrypted communication (HTTPS)

We make reasonable efforts to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, destruction, or disclosure.

6. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or transfer users' personal data to third parties for commercial purposes.

Data may be processed by trusted service providers solely for technical purposes such as:

  • hosting services
  • push notification delivery services (Expo, Apple, Google)
  • AI-based text extraction services (Google Cloud Vision, Google Gemini)
  • payment processing services (RevenueCat, Apple, Google)
  • usage analytics and statistics services (Firebase Analytics by Google, PostHog)
  • error and performance reporting services (Firebase Crashlytics)

Receipt images are processed through AI services (Google Cloud Vision, Google Gemini) exclusively for text extraction. Images are not permanently stored on our servers.

Premium subscription payments are processed through RevenueCat and Apple/Google. We do not have access to your card details.

These providers have access only to the data strictly necessary to provide their services and are required to maintain data confidentiality.

6.1. Analytics and session recording

To understand how the application is used and to continuously improve it, we use two analytics services:

Firebase Analytics (Google Ireland Limited)

Collects anonymous data about application usage:

  • screens visited and actions performed
  • feature usage frequency
  • error and performance reports
  • technical device information

Data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure in the European Union.

Firebase privacy policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

PostHog (PostHog Inc.)

Collects data about the user's interaction with the application, including session recordings. The recordings are similar to video materials showing navigation through the application, exclusively within the Fridgy app interface.

The recordings do NOT contain:

  • password field contents (automatically masked)
  • payment data (processed by Apple/Google in native dialogs, outside the application)
  • content from other applications on the device
  • camera or gallery images outside the application context

Only approximately 25% of sessions are recorded, randomly selected, to manage the volume of collected data.

Data is stored on PostHog Cloud infrastructure in the European Union (Frankfurt, Germany).

PostHog privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

Opt-out

Users may request the cessation of analytics data collection and session recording by contacting us at contact@fridgy.org. We will process the request within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR.

7. Data transfers outside the European Union

Personal data is primarily stored in the European Union. Our service providers (Firebase, PostHog, RevenueCat) use EU infrastructure for primary data storage.

In limited situations, certain technical data may be processed by partner services outside the EU (for example, for certain Google or Apple operations). In such cases, we ensure that adequate safeguards for data protection are applied, in accordance with Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) or other mechanisms provided by GDPR.

8. Push notifications

The Fridgy application may send push notifications related to the user's products and expiration dates.

Notifications are:

  • optional
  • can be disabled at any time from the application settings or device settings

9. Data retention period

Personal data is retained:

  • for as long as the user account exists
  • until the user deletes their account

After account deletion, the data is permanently removed from our systems, except where applicable legislation requires certain information to be retained for a specified period.

10. Users' rights

Under data protection legislation, users have the following rights:

  • the right to access their data
  • the right to rectify data
  • the right to delete their account and associated data
  • the right to withdraw consent
  • the right to request information regarding data processing

To exercise these rights, you may contact us at contact@fridgy.org.

Users also have the right to file a complaint with the competent data protection authority:

National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP)

Website: https://www.dataprotection.ro

11. Account deletion

Users may delete their account directly from the application, in the settings section.

By deleting the account:

  • all associated personal data is permanently removed
  • access to the application is revoked

12. Changes to the privacy policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy.

In the event of significant changes, users will be informed through the application or by other appropriate means.

The date of the last update will be displayed at the top of the document.

13. Contact
